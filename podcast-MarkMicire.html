Dr. Mark Micire — Head of Robotics, Woodside Energy

When I first heard the name Mark Micire it was in an announcement that he would be my next boss. When I first saw Mark Micire it was when I googled him and this intimidating photo came up. My immediate thought was "Oh no... we're getting a corporate American hard-ass". When Mark took over the team I quickly realised that he is one of the most easy going, down to earth, straight down the line guys I have ever met. Mark's resume speaks for itself, but that wouldn't make for a good podcast so we will be exploring his career life journey to date. It was a great pleasure to interview Mark and has been a long time coming. Enjoy the podcast.
